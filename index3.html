Housing Options & Cost

Traditional Dorms (Freshman)

Shared & single rooms in residence halls on North and South Campus. (DellPlain Hall / Ernie Davis Hall / Oren Lyons Hall / Shaw Hall / Watson Hall/ Day Hall / Flint Hall)

Cost: $5,760 (Split Room) - $6,170 (Single)

Advanced Housing (Sophomores)

Private or shared appartment with shared common areas for more space. (Milton Hall)

Cost: $7,210 (Single), $6,690 (Double), $6,220 (Triple), $8,200 (Qudruple)

Off-Campus Apartments (upperclassmen)

Options available nearby for upperclassmen and graduate students.

Cost: Varies by location
